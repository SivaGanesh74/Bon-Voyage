INDIA

The country is known for its rich cultural heritage, diverse geography, and historical landmarks, which attract tourists from all over the world. Additionally, India is home to several UNESCO World Heritage sites, exotic wildlife, and adventure tourism opportunities.

NEW ZEALAND

The main attraction of this place is its surreal natural beauty. Travellers and adventure lovers can gather amazing experiences from New Zealand. Apart from picturesque natural phenomena, New Zealand is also famous for its authentic Maori culture.

TURKEY

Tourism in Turkey is focused largely on a variety of historical sites, and on seaside resorts along its Aegean and Mediterranean Sea coasts. Turkey has also become a popular destination for culture, spa, and health care. Since 2021, Turkey is the fourth most visited country in the world.

ITALY

It is possibly one of the most sought-after destinations in the world when it comes to both quick holidays and long, slow vacations. It's also known for its many facets: the rich culture, the millenary history, the delicious cuisine and stunning and widely varied natural scenery.

SWITZERLAND

Tourism in Switzerland - Wikipedia Tourists are drawn to Switzerland's diverse landscape as well as the available activities, which take advantage of the Alpine climate and landscapes, in particular skiing and mountaineering. Switzerland is notable for its landscapes and tourism facilities (Matterhorn from the Gornergrat Railway).

FRANCE

France's scenery is as diverse as it is beautiful, with the glittering coastlines of the French Riviera, the patchwork fields of the Loire Valley, the snow-capped Alps and centuries old harbor towns of Normandy. This, of course, is hugely appealing for lovers of the great outdoors.
